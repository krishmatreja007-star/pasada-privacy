Pasada - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

What is Pasada?

Pasada is a personal, local-first productivity tool that helps product managers stay on top of their work across Google Workspace and Jira.

Data Collection

Pasada runs entirely on your local machine. No data is sent to any third-party server. All data retrieved from Google APIs is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device.

Google API Usage

Pasada accesses Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Chat, Google Docs, and Google Drive in read-only mode to surface relevant information and extract action items. Pasada does not modify, delete, or share any data from these services.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your machine. No data is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

Third-Party Services

Pasada sends text content to the Anthropic API (Claude) for AI-powered extraction. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed by their API.

Data Deletion

Delete the local SQLite database file at any time. Revoke access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
